03 / The Architect

A listening
practice.

I am an interior architect based in İstanbul, crafting environments that emerge from the dialogue between material honesty and poetic form. My work spans private residences, hospitality destinations, and institutional spaces — each shaped by a conviction that great interiors do not decorate a space, they complete it.

My approach begins with listening — to the client, to the light, to the site. From that listening, a spatial narrative emerges: grounded in material truth, refined through iterative form-finding, and delivered with the precision of technical excellence.

04 / The Studio YULA

YULA is the creative studio through which Melek Karlankuş brings her practice to life. The name carries a quiet resonance — and the circular forms within it echo the studio's recurring motif: wholeness, continuity, the complete arc from concept to construction. Based in İstanbul, working with clients across Turkey and internationally.
