Sarot Thermal Palace

Presidential Suite — Master Bedroom

Hospitality Interior 2023 Interior Design & Visualization

This project features the Master Bedroom design of the Presidential Suite at Sarot Thermal Palace. The space redefines 'thermal luxury' by harmonizing classical heritage with modern sophistication. The design is characterized by a deliberate contrast between ornate, gold-leaf motifs and the clean, rhythmic lines of contemporary fluted wood paneling. Every detail has been curated to create an exclusive, high-end sanctuary that honors the hotel's traditional roots while offering an avant-garde guest experience.

Challenge

Reconciling baroque grandeur with contemporary restraint in a single coherent space.

Solution

Gold-leaf ornament as accent, not pattern — isolated against clean fluted wood surfaces to create controlled contrast.

Outcome

A suite that feels both timeless and fresh; heritage without nostalgia.

Tools Used AutoCAD 3ds Max V-Ray Photoshop
