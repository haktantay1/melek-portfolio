Minimalist Living Space

Organic Form Meets Contemporary Luxury

Residential Interior 2024 Interior Design & Visualization

A sophisticated residential space where organic forms meet contemporary luxury. The project is defined by a custom-designed, fluid metallic fireplace that serves as a sculptural focal point. The design achieves a harmony between warm timber textures and minimalist Zen aesthetics, utilizing curved furniture and a carefully curated material palette to enhance the serene and refined atmosphere.

Challenge

Creating a space that feels simultaneously warm and minimal — avoiding coldness without sacrificing clarity.

Solution

A fluid metallic fireplace as the sculptural anchor, surrounded by organic terrazzo volumes and warm timber wall paneling.

Outcome

A residence that breathes — every element earns its place through material honesty and spatial poetry.

Tools Used AutoCAD 3ds Max V-Ray Photoshop SketchUp
