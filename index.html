№ 01 The Studio

We design bespoke rooms — residential, hospitality, and corporate spaces shaped by material honesty, drawn with technical precision, and delivered measured to the last line.

YULA Design Studio was founded in 2022 by interior architect Melek Karlankuş. The studio crafts immersive interiors that emerge from the dialogue between site, material, and use — from private residences in the Ida Mountains to corporate hubs in İstanbul. № 04 Four Commitments

Every room
begins with
four words.

01 / Listen.

Before any plan is drawn, the site speaks: orientation, light, the way air moves through a room. Design begins by recognizing what already exists — and what wants to be left alone.

02 / Specify.

Stone is stone, timber is timber. Every material is asked to be itself — selected for its grain, its weight, its age. Surface treatment is restraint, not disguise.

03 / Iterate.

The right curve doesn't arrive on the first sketch. It surfaces after many — through model studies, drawn sections, and the patient editing of every line until nothing extra remains.

04 / Deliver.

The drawing is a contract with the trade. Execution sets are produced so that what was imagined in the studio is what arrives on the site — measured, coordinated, buildable.
